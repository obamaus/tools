Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 26, 2025

Commitment to Privacy

At ObamaTools, we prioritize your privacy above all else. Our core service is designed to provide access to premium platforms through secure session sharing. We believe that your data belongs to you, and we have built our system to reflect this core value.

No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or monitor any personal data through our extension or dashboard. This includes:

  • Your browsing history
  • Your personal information (beyond your account credentials)
  • Your activities on any external websites
  • Your search queries or interactions

Purpose of the Extension

The ObamaTools browser extension serves a single, specific purpose: Session Synchronization. It allows our users to securely access shared premium accounts by synchronizing the necessary session tokens into your browser locally. This process is strictly one-way and happens entirely on your machine.

Security Measures

We use advanced encryption for all communication between our dashboard and the extension. Our "Secure Core" technology ensures that session data is never exposed to third parties and is cleared automatically.

Transparency

We are committed to being fully transparent about how our tools work. If you have any questions about our privacy practices or the technical implementation of our extension, please feel free to reach out to our team.
